"Straight Outta Hicktown"

2D Platformer developed in Unity

You play as B-Rad, a rapper from a small town trying to make it to the big city. Along the way he must overcome obstacles and gain enough confidence to overcome those who stand in his way.

This 2D Platformer was developed by my team and I for our "Game Mechanics" (Cmpt306) course project. It features original storyline and artwork as well as advanced AI that determines the difficulty of the boss based on the skill of the player, and each you encounter comes with their own unique behaviour.

Developed as a team by our group BroCode, we mainly stuck to assigned roles for developing (Animator, Artist, Programmer, Lead etc) but everyone had an opportunity to try them out. All of the artwork for the game was created by yours truly, and I also assisted in story elements, character design, level transitions, and basic enemy coding.
